I am a fifth-year Ph.D. candidate in Dr. Norma Fowler's lab at the University of Texas at Austin. My research interests revolve around plant community responses to wildland fire, and applying research towards land management and conservation goals. I use a combination of fieldwork, GIS, and statistical modeling to explore the relationships between pre- and post-fire plant communities.
