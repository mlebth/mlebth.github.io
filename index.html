I am a post-doctoral research fellow in Dr. Brent Sewall's lab at Temple University. My research interests revolve around plant community responses to wildland fire, fuel loading, and the effects of land management techniques on plant growth. I produce research with the intention of advancing our understanding of the ways in which fire affects the landscape, and of helping land managers reach conservation, restoration, and fire safety goals using a combination of fieldwork, GIS, and statistical modeling..
